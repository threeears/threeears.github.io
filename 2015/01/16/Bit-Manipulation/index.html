Bit Manipulation

I don’t use bit operation much in everyday life. But I know it’s very efficient in optimizing code.
So here I take down some of the best principles to remember from CTCI.

Basic Operation: ^ | &

Common Bit Tasks:

Clear the n rightmost bits of X.

Get ith bit of num

Set ith bit of num (to 1)

Clear ith bit of num

Clear from MSB(Most Significant Bit|High-Order Bit|Leftmost Bit) to ith bit of num

Clear from 0 to ith bit of num

Update ith bit of num, set its value to v
